Kosh — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Kosh is a personal finance app designed to work completely offline. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

Data Collection

Kosh does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There is no account, no login, and no backend. All your financial data (expenses, income, loans, goals) is stored exclusively on your device.

Permissions Used

  • Camera — Used only for scanning bills and receipts via the Bill Scanner feature. Photos are processed on-device and are never uploaded anywhere.
  • SMS (Read) — Used only to detect bank transaction messages for auto-logging expenses. SMS content is read locally and never transmitted.
  • Notifications — Used to send local budget alerts when you approach spending limits. No data leaves the device.
  • Biometric / Fingerprint — Used for the App Lock feature to secure access to the app. Biometric data is handled entirely by the Android system and is never accessed by Kosh.

Internet Access

Kosh requests internet permission solely to enable Google Play Services to download the optional OCR model for the Bill Scanner on first use. No personal or financial data is ever sent over the internet.

Third-Party Services

Kosh does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting services. No data is shared with any third party.

Data Storage

All app data is stored in your device's private internal storage. It is not included in cloud backups and cannot be accessed by other apps or file managers.

Children's Privacy

Kosh does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy
